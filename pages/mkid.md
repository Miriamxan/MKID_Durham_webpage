## What are MKIDs?
Microwave Kinetic Inductance Detectors (MKIDs) are superconducting detectors that allow for photon counting and energy resolution at millisecond time scales. 

They are key to the development of next-generation instruments such as **KIDSpec** and **MKID-CGI**.
